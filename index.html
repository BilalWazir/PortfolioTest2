Reflection

Building this website has been a truly rewarding experience for me. When I first started out, I wasn't sure how to approach the project or what to include in my portfolio. But as I began to gather my thoughts and ideas, the structure of the website started to take shape. I knew I wanted it to showcase my skills, interests, and projects while also making it user-friendly and visually appealing.

Throughout the process, I learned a lot about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I experimented with different design elements, such as gradients, animations, and hover effects. It took a bit of trial and error to get things just right, but with each adjustment, the website became better and more refined. One challenge I faced was making sure the site was responsive and looked good on mobile devices as well. I had to tweak some CSS properties to ensure the content was properly displayed on smaller screens.

I also had some fun adding my projects to the site. Figuring out how to display them with images and descriptions was an interesting task. I wanted the projects to be easily accessible, so I added links that launch the applications directly. This way, visitors can explore my work and get a feel for what I can do.

Creating the reflection section was a great opportunity to look back on my journey and take stock of what I've learned. Writing this reflection has made me realize just how much I've grown as a developer and how much I enjoy creating web experiences. I'm excited to continue learning and improving my skills, and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me next!
